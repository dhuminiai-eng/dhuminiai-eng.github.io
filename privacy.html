Privacy Policy

Dhumin AI Learning Solutions - Your Privacy Matters

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Dhumin AI Learning Solutions ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Key Points: We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy covers our data collection practices, how we use your information, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, address, city, state, country, postal code
  • Educational Information: School name, grade, board, and academic preferences
  • Contact Information: Phone number for SMS notifications and verification

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our Service:

  • Test Performance: Test attempts, scores, accuracy percentages, and learning progress
  • App Usage: Features used, time spent on different sections, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location information (city/state level) for analytics purposes

2.3 Communication Data

We collect information related to our communications with you:

  • Email Communications: Email logs, delivery status, and engagement metrics
  • SMS Communications: SMS logs, delivery status, and OTP verification records
  • Push Notifications: Notification preferences and delivery status

2.4 Payment Information

For subscription and test batch purchases, we collect:

  • Payment Details: Transaction amounts, payment methods, and billing information
  • Subscription Data: Plan details, billing periods, and payment history
  • Order Information: Purchase history and transaction records

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide and maintain our educational services

  • Generate test reports and performance analytics
  • Enable subscription management and billing

3.2 Communication

  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send educational content and study materials
  • Deliver test reminders and results

3.3 Personalization

  • Customize learning content based on your performance
  • Recommend relevant test batches and study materials
  • Adapt the user interface based on your preferences
  • Provide personalized study plans and recommendations

3.4 Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve our services
  • Conduct research to enhance educational effectiveness
  • Monitor system performance and identify technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular Audits: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, compliant cloud infrastructure

6.2 Data Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report the breach to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
  • Provide guidance on protective measures users can take
